TechCorp Inc. — Q3 2024 Earnings Summary
==========================================

QUARTERLY HIGHLIGHTS

Revenue: $1.15 billion (+14% YoY, +3% QoQ)
Gross Margin: 67% (up from 64% in Q3 2023)
Operating Income: $276 million (operating margin: 24%)
Net Income: $210 million
Earnings Per Share: $2.34 (diluted) — beat consensus estimate of $2.22 by $0.12
Free Cash Flow: $245 million

REVENUE BREAKDOWN BY SEGMENT

Cloud Services: $540M (+20% YoY)
- New customer acquisitions: 2,800 in Q3 (up 30% from Q3 2023)
- Multi-year deal bookings: $380M total contract value
- Cloud gross margin improved to 72% (from 68%)
- Key wins: Fortune 500 financial services firm ($15M ACV), European government agency ($8M ACV)

Software Licensing: $310M (+3% YoY)
- Enterprise agreement renewals: 92% renewal rate
- New licensing deals increasingly include cloud migration path
- Average deal size: $420K (up 8% YoY)

Professional Services: $180M (+10% YoY)
- Backlog: $450M (represents ~2.5 quarters of revenue)
- Utilization rate: 78% (target: 80%)
- Expanding AI implementation practice

Hardware: $120M (-5% YoY)
- Expected decline as product transitions continue
- IoT segment within hardware grew 18%

OPERATING METRICS

Customers >$100K ARR: 1,850 (up from 1,520 in Q3 2023)
Customers >$1M ARR: 185 (up from 142)
Dollar-Based Net Retention: 118%
Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) Payback: 14 months
LTV/CAC Ratio: 5.2x

BALANCE SHEET

Cash & Short-term Investments: $2.3 billion
Total Debt: $1.75 billion
Working Capital: $1.8 billion
Days Sales Outstanding (DSO): 52 days (improved from 58)
Deferred Revenue: $890M (+15% YoY)

GUIDANCE UPDATE — RAISED FY2024

Previous FY2024 Guidance: $4.1-4.3 billion revenue
Updated FY2024 Guidance: $4.2-4.3 billion revenue (raised low end)
Q4 2024 Revenue Guidance: $1.10-1.15 billion
FY2024 Operating Margin: 23-24% (raised from 22-23%)

CEO COMMENTARY (Sarah Chen)

"Q3 demonstrated the strength of our platform strategy. Cloud services grew 20% as enterprises accelerate digital transformation. Our AI capabilities are becoming a key differentiator — over 40% of new cloud deals now include our AI module. We're seeing strong traction in financial services and healthcare verticals."

"We raised our full-year guidance reflecting confidence in our pipeline. The backlog for professional services is at record levels, and our net retention rate of 118% shows customers are expanding their footprint on our platform."

CFO COMMENTARY (Michael Torres)

"Gross margins expanded 300 basis points year-over-year, driven by cloud mix shift and operational efficiency improvements. Free cash flow of $245 million reflects disciplined capital allocation. We returned $130 million to shareholders through buybacks in Q3 while maintaining a strong balance sheet with $2.3 billion in liquidity."
